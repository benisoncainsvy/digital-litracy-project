Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
1) Do’s
    • Maintain a professional and respectful online presence.
    • Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
    • Verify information before sharing it online.
    • Keep your personal information private and secure.
    • Use social media as a tool for networking and learning opportunities.
    • Report any suspicious or harmful content immediately.
    • Demonstrate ethical behavior.
2) Dont’s
    • Do not share personal details like phone number, OTP, or bank details.
    • Do not click unknown or suspicious links.
    • Do not spread false news or misinformation.
    • Do not use social media for bullying or harassment.
    • Do not share college or confidential details.
    • Do not accept unknown people’s friend requests.
    • Do not use weak or common passwords.
